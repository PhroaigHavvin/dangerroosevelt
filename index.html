About

Danger Roosevelt began in January 2017. After disbanding a previous band in December 2016, Justin Ewasko and Charlie Campo joined up with Ryan and Brandon Vasquez, who had previously played acoustically solo and together, to form a new rock band. Filling out the line-up with Angel Costilla, who had also been playing regular with another band, they began writing together. Pulling first from solo acts, previous bands, and covers as inspiration, Danger Roosevelt soon found their sound and began recording what would become their first demo.

With 11 songs recorded and due to be release as a demo in early 2018, the band hopes to embark on a city-wide tour throughout 2018.

Line-up

  • Ryan Vasquez - Rhythm/Lead Guitar, Vocals
  • Brandon Vasquez - Keys, Vocals, Backing Guitar
  • Justin Ewasko - Lead Guitar, Backing Vocals
  • Angel Costilla - Bass, Tone
  • Charlie Campo - Drums, Percussion
